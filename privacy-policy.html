Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 7, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to THG. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information on our website. Your privacy is important to us.

Information We Collect

This website is a static portfolio and does not personally collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data such as your name, email address, or location.

  • Language Preference: To enhance your browsing experience, we use your browser's `localStorage` to remember your selected language (English or Arabic). This information is stored only on your own device and is not sent to us or any server.
  • No Cookies: We do not use cookies for tracking or data collection.

Third-Party Services

Our website contains links to third-party websites and services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these other sites. When you follow a link to an external site, we encourage you to read their privacy policy.

  • Hosting: This site is hosted on GitHub Pages.
  • Fonts: We use Google Fonts to display our website's typography.
  • External Links: We link to services such as PayPal, Instagram, Facebook, and YouTube. These services have their own separate privacy policies.

Data Security

We take security seriously. Our website is served over HTTPS, which encrypts the connection between your browser and our server. As we do not collect personal information, the risk of data exposure from our site is minimal.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
